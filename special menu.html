Tteokbokki

Tteokbokki is a very famous streetfood in Korea. It is ricecakes cooked in some type of special and spicy sauce.

Kimchi Fried Rice

Kimchi Fried Rice is a type of Fried Rice but it's fried with Kimchi and you also put in some meat to fry it along with the rice. Then after you fry it, you season it with some sesame, seaweed and an egg and that's a perfect Kimchi Fried Rice.
