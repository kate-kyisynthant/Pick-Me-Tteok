About Pick Me Tteok

What's Pick Me Tteok?

Pick Me Tteok is a Korean restaurant that is trying to open all around the world to make people enjoy and recognize the taste of Korean food.

What's the purpose of our restaurant?

The purpose of our restaurant is to make people know how Korean food tastes like and enjoy them no matter where they are around the world

How did we get the idea to name our restaurant Pick Me Tteok?

We three were just discussing about what to name our restaurant when we got to the topic of 'Pick Me Girls' and we were like "What if we just named it 'Pick Me Tteok'?" and basically that was how it was created. At first we had some thoughts with the name 'Angry Tofu' or 'Pick Me Tofu' but we thought Tteok represented the food in Korea more so we just went with it.
